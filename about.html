About Closet.io !
The "Virtual Wardrobe App" is a web-based platform that helps users manage their wardrobe and create outfits virtually. The platform will allow users to upload images of their clothing items and create virtual outfits for different occasions, while also providing suggestions for new items to add to their wardrobe based on their personal style and preferences.

Features:


Closet organization:

Organize your clothes by categories such as tops, bottoms, dresses, shoes, and accessories.

Photo upload:

Take or upload photos of their clothes and accessories to the Website.

Outfit creation:

Create outfits by combining items in their virtual wardrobe

Calendar integration:

Plan outfits for specific events by integrating your virtual wardrobe with your calendar

Weather integration:

Information on the weather forecast, so you can plan your outfits accordingly

Style suggestions:

Suggests outfit combinations based on the your preferences, current trends, and fashion rules

Wishlist creation:

Create a wishlist of clothes and accessories you would like to add to your virtual wardrobe.

Sharing:

Plan outfits for specific events by integrating your virtual wardrobe with your calendar

Analytics:

Provide you with analytics on your wardrobe

Shopping:

Provide you with the option to shop for new clothes and accessories directly from the app.
